Instant Noodles

Description

Cup Noodles are your classic "I'm feeling lazy" meal! With few ingredients and short cooking time, Cup Noodles are the quintessential degen meal!

Ingredients

  • Cup Noodles
  • Water
  • Chopsticks or similar utensil
  • Functional microwave
  • No life

Steps

  1. Retrieve materials
  2. Peel back half of the Cup Noodles lid
  3. Pour water in until it reaches the inner line
  4. Place cup in microwave and microwave for the amount of time recommended
  5. CONSUME
